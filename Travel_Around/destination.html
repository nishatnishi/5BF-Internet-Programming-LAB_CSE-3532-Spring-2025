Popular Destinations

Cox's Bazar

Location: Cox's Bazar Sea Beach, Bangladesh

Cox’s Bazar, located in the southeastern region of Bangladesh, is famous for the world's longest unbroken sandy sea beach. The serene environment and picturesque views make it a prime tourist destination. Whether you're looking to relax, enjoy water sports, or explore the nearby hills, Cox's Bazar offers something for everyone.

Best Time to Visit:The best time to visit Cox's Bazar is generally between November and March when the weather is pleasant, dry, and ideal for beach activities, with temperatures ranging from 20°C to 30°C

Location: Inani Beach, Cox's Bazar

Inani Beach is located around 30 km south of Cox’s Bazar. Known for its clear blue waters and beautiful coral stones, Inani is less crowded than the main Cox's Bazar beach, offering a more tranquil and private experience. It is an ideal spot for nature lovers and those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of the city.

Best Time to Visit: November to March

Location: Himchari Fountain and Hill, Cox's Bazar

Himchari is a famous hilltop located just 12 km south of Cox's Bazar. It offers a spectacular view of the Bay of Bengal and the nearby beaches. The hill is known for its waterfalls, which are especially breathtaking during the monsoon season. It’s a perfect spot for nature walks and photography.

Best Time to Visit: November to March

Saint Martin

Location: Saint Martin Island, Bangladesh

Saint Martin Island is the only coral island in Bangladesh. It is located in the Bay of Bengal and is renowned for its crystal-clear water, stunning coral reefs, and vibrant marine life. The island is a paradise for beach lovers, and visitors can enjoy water activities such as snorkeling, diving, and boat rides.

Best Time to Visit: November to March

Location: Sand Castle Resort, Saint Martin

Sand Castle Resort is a luxurious retreat located on the edge of Saint Martin Island. It offers pristine views of the beach, cozy cottages, and an exceptional stay with modern amenities. It's an ideal place for a romantic getaway or a peaceful family vacation.

Location: Josnaloy Resort, Saint Martin

Josnaloy Beach Resort is located near the serene beach of Saint Martin. This resort offers a peaceful and eco-friendly environment, perfect for those looking to connect with nature. Guests can enjoy activities like beach walks, fishing, and exploring the island's rich biodiversity.

Dhaka

Location: Ahsan Manzil,Old Dhaka

Ahsan Manzil is a historic palace in Dhaka, once home to the Nawabs. Now a museum, it offers a glimpse into the rich history and culture of Bangladesh. The pink palace, with its stunning architecture and ornate interiors, attracts visitors who wish to learn about Bangladesh's royal past.

Best Time to Visit:You can visit Ahsan Manzil, the Pink Palace, in Dhaka, from Wednesday to Monday, from 10:30 AM to 5:30 PM (October to March) and from 10:30 AM to 6:00 PM (April to September), as it is closed on Tuesdays.

Location:Lalbagh Road, Dhaka 1211

Lalbagh Fort is one of the key tourist attractions of Dhaka City in Bangladesh.It is a historic site in Dhaka, built during the Mughal era. It was started in 1678 under the leadership of Dara Shikoh, the son of Mughal emperor Aurangzeb, though it remains incomplete to this day. The fort is known for its unique architecture, rich history, and mysterious ambiance, making it a popular destination for history enthusiasts and tourists alike. It is the most popular and renowned fort and a great art signature by the Mughal Empire in Bangladesh. It is also known as Fort Aurangabad.For anyone visiting Bangladesh,Lalbagh Fort is one of the must-visiting attractions.

Best Time to Visit:You can visit Lalbagh Fort in Dhaka, Bangladesh, every day except Sundays and public holidays, with the best time to visit being during the cooler months (October to March).

Location:Sarah Resort,Gazipur

Sarah Resort, located in Gazipur, is a beautiful and peaceful place to relax. Surrounded by green trees and nature, it offers comfortable rooms and fun activities. It’s a great spot for family trips and company events, providing a calm and refreshing environment for everyone to enjoy.

Chittagong

Location: Patenga Sea Beach, Chittagong

Patenga Beach is a popular destination in Chittagong, offering stunning views of the Bay of Bengal. The beach is surrounded by lush greenery, and visitors can enjoy local street food, boat rides, and peaceful walks along the shore. It’s a perfect spot for families and couples alike.

Best Time to Visit:The best time to visit Patenga Sea Beach in Chittagong, Bangladesh, is during the cooler and drier months, from November to March, when the weather is mild and pleasant.

Location: Karnaphuli River, Chittagong

Karnaphuli River is a major river in Chittagong, known for its natural beauty and tranquility. Visitors can take boat rides to explore the river, enjoy the picturesque landscapes, and visit nearby attractions like the Kaptai Dam and hill tracts.

Best Time to Visit:During the dry season, which typically falls between November and February.

Location: Foy's Lake, Chittagong

Foy's Lake is a man-made lake situated in Chittagong, surrounded by beautiful hills and forests. It’s an ideal location for boat rides, picnics, and spending time with family and friends. The lake offers a serene and peaceful atmosphere away from the city's noise.

Best Time to Visit:09:00 am - 06:00 pm

Rangamati

Location: Hanging Bridge, Kaptai, Rangamati

The Hanging Bridge over the Kaptai Lake in Rangamati is an iconic spot offering breathtaking views of the lake and surrounding hills. It is an adventure spot for thrill-seekers and a perfect place to capture some stunning photographs.

Best Time to Visit:November to February. During these months, the weather remains relatively cool and dry, making it perfect for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

Location: shuvolong Waterfall, Rangamati

Shuvolong Waterfall is a beautiful waterfall in Rangamati. The only available path to reach the waterfall is through the lake. People visit there by boat. It is a famous place with tourists for the waterfall, high hills, and the nature surrounding it offers.

Best Time to Visit:The best time to visit Shuvolong Waterfall in Rangamati, Bangladesh, is during the rainy season (monsoon), specifically between late monsoon (September to December), when the waterfall is at its fullest and most impressive.

Location: Sajek Valley, Rangamati

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Sajek Valley, currently the most popular destination for pilgrims/travelers, is located in the biggest union of Bangladesh, Baghaichari Upazila of Rangamati district. The Sajek Valley Tour is the ideal to watch the fluttering hilly clouds like natural landscapes and cotton.

Best Time to Visit: In the Rainy, Autumn, and Late autumn, you will feel the white clouds very closely. So, it is considered as the best time to visit Sajek Valley. There is no problem if you go another time to travel to Sajek.

Bandarban

Location: Nilgiri Hill, Bandarban

Nilgiri or Nil Giri is one of the tallest peaks and beautiful tourist spot in Bangladesh. It is about 3500 feet high and situated on the hill side in Bandarban District, Surrounded by beautiful hilly natural views and clouds most of the time.

Best Time to Visit:The best time to visit Nilgiri in Bandarban, which is one of the highest peaks in Bangladesh, is during the dry season, which starts from October and extends through to February.

Location: Sangu River,Bandarban

Sangu River is the complete part of the natural beauty of Bandarban. It follows a northerly circuitous course in the hill tracts up to Bandarban. It has huge financial interests for the population of Bandarban.

Best Time to Visit: during the dry season, which usually spans from November to early April. During this period, the weather is mild with reduced humidity and relatively cool temperatures

Location: Amiakhum Waterfall, Bandarban

Amiakhum Waterfall is a magnificent waterfall located in Thanchi upazila of Bandarban district. It is one of the most inaccessible places in Bangladesh. The stream of cold water is coming down at a tremendous speed, spreading milky white foam over the green hills and stone barriers.It Surrounded by dense forests, this majestic waterfall offers a serene and peaceful atmosphere, making it a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts and trekkers.

Best Time to Visit:In the rainy season, the beauty of Amiakhum regains its fullness.

Sylhet

Location: sreemangal, Sylhet

Jaflong is a beautiful hill station in Sylhet, known for its tea gardens, waterfalls, and breathtaking views of the Khasi hills. The area is a popular destination for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful retreat amidst lush greenery.

Best Time to Visit:November to March (Winter/Dry Season)

Location: Jaflong, Sylhet

The shrine of Hazrat Shah Jalal, a prominent Sufi saint, is a famous religious site in Sylhet. The peaceful environment and the architectural beauty of the shrine attract thousands of visitors every year, making it one of the most important landmarks of Sylhet.

Best Time to Visit:If you are interested in the natural beauty of Jaflong, the best time to visit will be during the monsoon (July-September). If you are interested in photographing the stone collecting activities, you need to visit during winter (October to May).

Location: lalakhal,Sylhet

Sylhet is famous for its sprawling tea estates. The rolling hills and scenic tea gardens make for a picturesque landscape. Visitors can explore the gardens, learn about tea processing, and enjoy the refreshing atmosphere of this region.

Best Time to Visit: November to March, when the weather is pleasant and the water is clear, offering ideal conditions for sightseeing and boat rides.

Khulna

Location: Sundarbans Mangrove Forest,Khulna

The Sundarbans is the world's largest contiguous mangrove forest, spanning approximately 10,000 square kilometers (3,900 sq mi) across the Ganges Delta in India and Bangladesh, and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. it is a popular tourist destination known for its rare tigers,breathtaking natural beauty and diverse wildlife.

Best Time to Visit:The best time to visit Sundarbans is during the winter months, from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and the animals are more active.

Location: Sixty Dome Mosque,Bagerhat,Khulna

The Sixty Dome Mosque, also known as the Shat Gombuj Mosque, is located in Bagerhat, a city in southwestern Bangladesh. It is one of the most significant historical landmarks in the region, known for its architectural beauty and historical significance. The mosque was built in the 15th century and is part of the Bagerhat UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Location:Rupsha River,Khulna

The Rupsha River is a picturesque destination in Khulna, Bangladesh, known for its serene beauty and the iconic Rupsha Bridge. Rupsa River, located in the Khulna district near the Sundarbans, is renowned for its calm, clear waters and scenic surroundings.

Best Time to Visit:the best time to visit is during the dry season, which spans from November to March. During these months, the weather is generally cooler and less humid, with clear skies that are ideal for photography and comfortable cruising on the river.
